CSC 362: Data Visualization

This page includes lab projects from my Computer Science 362 class, in which we explored data visualization through D3.js and interactive web development.

For our final project, my lab partner and I built a visualization in D3 using data from Yale's Project on Climate Change that allows a user to visualize how opinions about climate change in America have changed over the past decade and a half. Click here to see the visualization and here to read our writeup on the work we did.
